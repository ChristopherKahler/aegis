<objective>
## Goal
Create 6 infrastructure domain knowledge modules covering the foundational and operational layers of codebase auditing: Context & Intent, Architecture & System Design, Data & State Integrity, Reliability & Resilience, Scalability & Performance, and Operability & Developer Experience.

## Purpose
Domain modules are the subject matter knowledge that agents apply their personas against. Without domains, agents have no structured knowledge of what to look for, what failure patterns exist, or what correct patterns look like. These 6 infrastructure domains cover the system-level concerns that every codebase audit must address — from understanding what the system is supposed to do (Domain 00) through whether it can sustain operations under real-world conditions (Domains 07, 08, 10).

## Output
- 6 domain files in `src/domains/`
- Each with 8 sections: Overview, Audit Questions, Failure Patterns, Best Practice Patterns, Red Flags, Tool Affinities, Standards & Frameworks, Metrics
</objective>
